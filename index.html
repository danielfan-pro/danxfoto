About

Hi, I'm Dan

I'm a photographer based in Melrose, MA and serving Boston and the Greater Boston Area, with a passion for capturing the moments that matter most — the quiet glances, the joyful tears, the unscripted laughter. Every family has a story worth telling, and I'm here to tell yours.

I specialize in family and portrait photography, bringing both technical skill and an artistic eye to every session. My approach is relaxed, personal, and always focused on capturing your unique moments.
